About Us

M/S JEETTECNIKA is a professionally managed partnership firm registered under the Indian Partnership Act, 1932. Established on 24th November 2020, headquartered in Saran, Bihar, we specialize in innovative, sustainable, and high-quality engineering, maintenance, and industrial service solutions for Indian Railways and allied industries.

We are located near Rail Wheel Plant (RWP), Bela and are a trusted partner to enhance operational efficiency while adhering to RDSO, ICF, and RWP Bela standards.
