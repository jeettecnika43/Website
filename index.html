About Us

M/S JEETTECNIKA is a professionally managed partnership firm, established on November 24, 2020, and registered under the Indian Partnership Act, 1932. Headquartered in Saran, Bihar, near the strategic Rail Wheel Plant (RWP), Bela, JEETTECNIKA has rapidly emerged as a trusted and innovative engineering solutions provider, specializing in comprehensive services for Indian Railways and allied industries.

Our firm is founded on a vision to revolutionize the railway and industrial maintenance domain by blending traditional engineering expertise with modern technology and sustainable practices. We focus on delivering high-quality, reliable, and eco-friendly solutions across electrical, mechanical, and civil engineering disciplines.

Nature of Business

M/S JEETTECNIKA is engaged in delivering comprehensive and integrated engineering solutions tailored to meet the dynamic needs of the railway and industrial sectors. Our core business activities encompass the following:

Turnkey Contract Services

Execution of end-to-end project delivery for railway and industrial clients, including planning, execution, and commissioning of electrical, mechanical, and civil engineering works. We ensure seamless coordination and timely completion, maintaining the highest quality standards.

Annual Maintenance Contracts (AMC)

Provision of preventive and corrective maintenance services for electrical and mechanical panels, machinery, and allied facilities. Through scheduled inspections, timely repairs, and effective asset management, we optimize operational uptime and extend equipment life.

Customized Industrial Engineering Solutions

Development and deployment of client-specific engineering services, including on-site technical support, process optimization, and innovative problem-solving to meet unique operational challenges. Our approach integrates cutting-edge technologies with practical expertise.

Authorized Representative and Liaison Services

Acting as the trusted partner and official liaison for reputed brands and suppliers within the railway and industrial ecosystem. We facilitate smooth procurement, technical coordination, and supply chain management to ensure availability of high-quality materials and components.

Supply Chain and Materials Management

Reliable sourcing and timely delivery of electrical, mechanical, and civil components, including specialized refractory products crucial to railway manufacturing operations. Our robust vendor relationships and quality control mechanisms ensure consistent product standards.

Technical Consultation and Support

Offering expert advisory services for project planning, engineering designs, compliance audits, and regulatory adherence, helping clients navigate complex technical requirements and improve operational efficiency.
